Why this exists

  • 22 states have USDA-approved SNAP food restrictions rolling out 2026–2028.
  • Rules vary wildly — Iowa's ban is huge, West Virginia's is narrow.

  • SnapScan checks before the register — no awkward moments, no go-backs.

    How SnapScan works

    1. 1

      Pick your state

      Or let SnapScan auto-detect it. Each state's SNAP rules load instantly.

    2. 2

      Scan the barcode

      Your phone camera reads the UPC using ZXing — fully on-device, no photos uploaded.

    3. 3

      Get an instant verdict

      We look up the product in Open Food Facts and match it against your state's restricted categories.

    Install as an app

    On iPhone: tap Share → "Add to Home Screen". On Android: tap the menu → "Install app". Works offline after first load.
